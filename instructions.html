Echo Maze

Use W, A, S, D to move.

To send out a ping, click and drag in the direction that you want to ping. (The starting point and the length of the drag are not important, only the angle).

You will hear a tone when your ping goes out and when it encounters an obstacle. Use the time between these pings to figure out the distance to an obstacle in that direction.

If the pitch of the response ping is lower, then your ping has reached a wall. If the pitch is higher, however, you have found your goal!

If you walk into an obstacle, you will here a muffled, lower tone.
